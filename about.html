About Us:

At Little Tails, we are passionate about providing pets related services that allow users to connect to get a pet, look for a new family for their pet and find a partner for their beloved dogs and cats in their locality. We believe that every pet deserves a loving home and we are here to help make that happen.

Mission:

Our mission at Little Tails is to create a platform where pet owners can easily connect with others in their community to provide the best care and companionship for their furry friends.

Vision:

Our vision at Little Tails is to become the go-to destination for all pet-related needs, where users can find everything they need to ensure the happiness and well-being of their pets.

Core Values:

  • Compassion: We believe in treating all pets with love and respect.
  • Community: We strive to create a supportive and inclusive community of pet owners.
  • Convenience: We are committed to providing a user-friendly platform that makes pet care easy and accessible.
